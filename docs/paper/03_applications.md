## 28. Physics

### Coherence Under Constraint in Fundamental Systems

#### Crystal Formation

Atoms in a solid arrange into lattice structures.

- Dynamics: thermal motion, electromagnetic interaction  
- Constraints: bond energies, spatial geometry  

Below a critical temperature, atoms settle into a coherence basin representing the lattice.

Result:
- synchronized atomic vibrations  
- geometry locks structure in place  

**CUC interpretation:**
dynamic atoms + electromagnetic constraints → coherent lattice structure

---

#### Superconductivity

Electrons form Cooper pairs, creating a coherent quantum state.

- Constraints: lattice structure, temperature threshold  

Below critical temperature:
- electron phases synchronize  
- resistance drops to zero  

---

#### Bose–Einstein Condensates

At extremely low temperatures:
- particle wavefunctions overlap  
- system enters a unified quantum state  

Constraint:
- near-zero thermal energy
